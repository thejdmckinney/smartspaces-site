SmartSpaces DFW was founded on a simple principle: smart homes should actually work reliably. Too many homeowners invest in smart technology only to deal with constant connectivity issues, devices that won't talk to each other, and frustrating technical problems.

Our network health first approach sets us apart. Before we install a single smart device, we assess and optimize your home's Wi-Fi infrastructure. This ensures every device has the strong, stable connection it needs to perform reliably.

We exclusively use proven, professional-grade products from brands like Home Assistant, Ubiquiti UniFi, Lutron, and Ecobee. These aren't the consumer gadgets you find at big box stores - they're the same reliable systems used in commercial installations.
